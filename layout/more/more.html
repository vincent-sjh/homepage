Programming Game
2022.11 ~ 2023.1

In this game, players need to input code to control a robot to complete specified tasks, which effectively trains programming thinking without requiring knowledge of any specific programming language,as the game uses its own easy-to-understand code format. LLM (Large Language Model) LCA (Life Cycle Assessment) carbon footprint measurement and carbon reduction strategies
Blog
2023.9 ~ 2024.01
I led a team to evaluate the LCA carbon footprint of large language models (LLMs) and proposed corresponding carbon reduction strategies. Our calculation formula takes into account both the operational and embodied footprints, combining the Scaling Law and the FLOP model, with estimations based on model performance across various validation sets. The formula we summarized is
\( CO_2^{eq}=CO_2eq_{oper}+CO_2eq_{emb} \approx \frac {n_{dev} \cdot t_{train} \cdot CO2_{eq_{chip}}}{\text{lifetime}} + \frac{FLOPS \cdot W_{peak} \cdot PUE \cdot carbon_{inten}}{FLOP_{peak}} \).
Our findings indicate that sparse Mixture of Experts (MoE) architectures‚Äîthe core idea of MoE being to decompose a large model into multiple smaller models (experts), with a gating network deciding which experts to activate for each input sample‚Äîand the use of specialized ML hardware, such as energy-efficient TPUs instead of GPUs, can significantly reduce carbon emissions while maintaining performance metrics. These are worthwhile measures for carbon reduction.
