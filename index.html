I am a senior undergraduate student from Zhejiang University majoring in Information Security in Turing Class, first honors class at Chu Kochen Honors College, and I am currently ranked first in my major. I minor in the Honors Program of Innovation and Entrepreneurship, the best minor program at Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University.

During my undergraduate studies, I am grateful to be advised by Prof. Bingsheng Zhang. I also had a pleasant summer at Yale University in 2023, under the guidance of Prof. Fan Zhang.

I'm a hacker, creator, and entrepreneur, inspired by "Hackers and Painters" by Y Combinator founder Paul Graham. Hacking, or developing, is the act of creating something new, much like painting. This analogy eventually became my guiding principle. My computer is like my paintbrush, I use it to "paint" my products, blogs and videos. Hacking is a way for me to express myself, connect, and resonate with others.

I am currently interested in generative AI, crypto, and HCI. At the same time, I am also interested in their applications in gaming, education, and social scenarios. I am currently leading a team to explore the future of education, focusing on creating AI-powered gamified PBL (Project-Based Learning) software on the iPad for elementary school students. The software will help children to use generative AI tools to complete their projects within the game.
